Written report (50%) 
You must structure your report covering the following fundamental points: 
A. Model specification and estimation: Formulate and estimate at least two discrete choice 
models (e.g., a base Multinomial Logit Model and an advanced specification including 
interaction terms or latent variables). Clearly define the utility functions for your alternatives. 
Present your estimation results in a clean, professional table and discuss the goodness-of-fit 
measures (e.g., Log-Likelihood, Rho-squared) to justify your preferred model.

B. Parameter interpretation and behavioral insights: Analyze the estimated coefficients 
of your preferred model. Are the signs intuitive and theoretically correct? Are the parameters 
statistically significant? Discuss what these values reveal about consumer preferences in your

C. Scenario analysis and prediction: Using your preferred model, design and simulate at 
least two realistic "what-if" scenarios (e.g., a 20% price increase by a competitor, the

into the real world. Based on your calculations and scenario analyses, provide highly specific,
